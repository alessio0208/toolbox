               <h1>Centres</h1>

 	
	

           



      <p>Groundbreaking research increasingly requires to cross disciplinary boundaries and to tear down thought silos. Scientists of multidisciplinary backgrounds more and more work together in interdisciplinary teams to address today’s grand challenges and research questions.</p>

<h2>Three interdisciplinary centres</h2><p>The University of Luxembourg has set up three interdisciplinary centres (IC) <b>to ease the cooperation between different scientific disciplines </b>and <b>to exploit the benefits of collaborative research</b>. The ICs bring together teaching and research on cross-cutting issues in several disciplines promoting innovative approaches and new perspectives.</p>

<p>This interdisciplinary approach helps to increase Luxembourg’s footprint by achieving high credibility and international recognition in the academic world. It fosters interaction between academia and industry in the country.</p>
